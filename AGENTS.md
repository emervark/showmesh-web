## Design requirements

Before changing any UI, read and follow:

- `docs/SHOWMESH_UI_UX_DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md`

These rules are mandatory, not inspirational.

Do not introduce:
- generic SaaS landing-page patterns;
- oversized multi-line hero typography;
- decorative gradients or glows;
- claim pills or badge clouds;
- lime accent colors;
- cropped or illegible product UI;
- “QLab for Windows” positioning.

When a design decision is not covered, preserve the visual language of the supplied Showmesh application UI. Do not invent a new visual direction.
